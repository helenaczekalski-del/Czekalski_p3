Digital Swatches - Instructions

Desc: participants will use colored in tape and overlap them to make a colorful design Tool list: marker and tape and paper Time estimate: ten minutes Any number of participants There are two jobs, coloring in the tape (job 1) and placing the tape (job 2)

  • if (multiple participants)
  • then {split into two groups}
  • if (group 1)
  • then {start with job 1}
  • if (group 2)
  • then {start job 2}
  • if (five minutes have passed)
  • then {switch jobs}
  • if (doing job 1)
  • then {cut tape of any length and color on tape with any color}
  • if (doing job 2)
  • then {place tape anywhere on paper}
